Noodle Sandwich

Description

Wow your friends and family at the dinner table with this unorthodox twist on a staple dinner. Takes only 4-5 minutes of preparation per serving and guarantees to leave you guests speechless. Momofuku Ando from Ritsumeikan University in Japan is accredited as being the inventor of the instant ramen we know and love today, many believing the invention to be the greatest thing since sliced bread, which leads us to our next ingredient. Otto Fredrerick Rohwedder of Davenport, Iowa, United States, created the Yang to the Ying for our recipe so to speak. He invented the first single loaf bread-slicing machine and laid the groundwork for one of the most groundbreaking food combinations known to man.

Ingredients

  • One packet of your favorite instant noodles
  • One loaf of bread
  • One glass of water

Steps

  1. Follow instructions on pack of noodles so that they are prepared correctly according to their brand
  2. Add prepared noodles from cup if needed into a bowl to add to the presentation of the dish.
  3. (Optional) Drain broth from bowl by tipping bowl downwards into the sink, use a fork to hold noodles firmly in place.
  4. (Optional Remove crust pieces from loaf of bread.
  5. Serve meal with loaf of bread and glass of water within arms reach of noodles. During service the person eating will be able to decide how many slices of bread they would like to use, it's most common to use a fork to set noodles in place on the bread while flat, then to fold.
