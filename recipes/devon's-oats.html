Devons Famous Oats

Description

This coco pops inspired take on the classic microwaved oats has taken the world by storm, being used by bodybuilders and fitness enthusiasts to enjoy a delicious meal after work without having to feel guilty afterwards. The exact origin of this meal is of some debate but most expert oatologists believe this variation on microwaved oats is a derivative of Devon Kahika-Campbell's oats first made in Kawerau, New Zealand in the early 21st century.

Ingredients

  • Rolled oats - 50g
  • Water - 2 cups
  • Peanut butter - 1 Tblsp
  • Almonds - 1 Handful
  • Chocolate weigh protein powder - 1 scoop
  • Chia seeds - 2 tsp
  • Frozen berries (mixed) - 1 Handful
  • One banana

Steps

  1. Add oats to a large bowl add water and microwave according to instructions on packet of oats. (2-4 minutes recommended)
  2. Remove oats from microwave, add peanut butter, and mix until no clumps remain.
  3. Add protein powder and mix until oats are indistinguishable from coco pops.
  4. Add chia seeds and mix.
  5. Add almonds, mixing is personal preference
  6. Cover oats with layer of mixed berries.
  7. Slice banana (Optional). Place banana on top of mixed berry layer.
  8. Serve.
